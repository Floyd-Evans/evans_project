Here is my Family

Granted, they are grown up now and no longer this small. We decided to go for a stroll that day. Pictured are five of my six. From left to right: Mitzi, Abbagail, Angelina, Floyd Jr., and Tabitha.

For the most part, we have gotten used to the crazy Iowa weather. Warm and sunny one day, then freezing rain or snow the next. I moved here from New York City and do not plan on going back. Iowa is friendly and a great place to raise a family or simply slow down and grow some corn.
